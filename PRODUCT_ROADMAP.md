# Product Roadmap & Functionality Gaps

This document identifies the critical functionality gaps required to transition the Workly platform from a functional technical foundation into a highly successful, production-ready hyper-local marketplace.

## 1. Trust & Safety (High Priority) [COMPLETED]
*   **KYC & Identity Verification:** Automated identity verification (e.g., matching government ID with selfies) for Help Providers before they can accept jobs.
*   **Ratings & Reviews System:** A robust two-way rating system (Seekers rating Providers and vice versa) with text reviews. Needed for quality control and trust.
*   **Dispute Resolution/Support Ticketing:** A workflow for users to report issues (e.g., job not completed, payment disputes, behavioral issues) integrated with the Admin Portal.
*   **Number Masking & In-App Calling:** Privacy protection to prevent direct off-platform communication and harassment using VoIP or telephony APIs. 

## 2. Payments & Monetization (High Priority) [COMPLETED]
*   **Payment Gateway Integration:** Integration with Stripe, Razorpay, or similar for capturing payments securely from Seekers. 
*   **Escrow / Wallet System:** Holding Seeker funds in escrow upon job start and releasing them to the Provider upon verified job completion.
*   **Dynamic Pricing & Bidding:** Support for fixed-price jobs, hourly rate tracking, or a bidding mechanism where Providers submit quotes for a Seeker's job request.
*   **Commission & Payout Management:** Automated platform fee deduction and scheduled periodic payouts to Providers' bank accounts.

## 3. Core Operational Features (Medium Priority) [COMPLETED]
*   **Scheduled Booking:** Expansion of the current real-time job matching engine to allow scheduling services for an exact future date/time.
*   **Real-time Location Tracking:** Live map tracking of the Provider's location (ETA) once they have accepted a job and are en route to the Seeker's location.
*   **Provider Availability Management:** A calendar or toggle system for Providers to set daily working hours, holidays, and temporary offline states to avoid missed requests.
*   **Job Rescheduling & Cancellation Policies:** Enforcement of cancellation fees and automated rescheduling logic for both Seekers and Providers.

## 4. Growth & Retention (Medium to Low Priority)
*   **Promotions & Referral Program:** A promotional code engine and referral link tracking to incentivize user acquisition.
*   **Tiered Provider Programs:** "Super Provider" badges based on completion rate and ratings, giving them priority access to higher-paying jobs or exclusive requests.
*   **Localized Pricing / Surge Pricing:** Algorithmic adjustments of base prices based on local demand levels, weather events, or traffic conditions.

---
*Note: The upcoming services discussed above will be integrated into the existing distributed architecture step-by-step.*
